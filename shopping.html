Destination Leeds

Trinity Leeds

March 18, 2019




Trinity Leeds is hard to miss! It houses over 120 brands and a variety of restaurants and cafes. The striking feature of Trinity Leeds, is its food area: the "Trinity Kitchen" which offers food from various cuisines! The concept of the kitchen is that every 8 weeks, 6 new street food vans will be available who will be selling their food alonsgside permanent food vendors such as Rola Wala, Pizzaluxe and Absurd Bird.

Additional Information

  • Address: Brownberrie Ln, Horsforth, Leeds LS18 5HD



Victoria Leeds

March 18, 2019




If you've got a taste for high-end fashion and beauty, and fine dining, then look no further than Victoria Leeds. Victoria Leeds combines the Victoria Quarter (1990) and the Victoria Gate shopping centre (2016) to bring you the best shopping experience. It has a casino and carries large retailers like Harvey Nichols and John Lewis.

  • Address: Victoria Leeds, Queen Victoria St LS1 6BE


Queens Arcade

March 18, 2019


Do you enjoy aesthetic decor and independent shops? If so, then Queens Arcade sounds like your calling! Come and walk through this arcade if you want to have a little browse, escape the typical British shower, or even just avoid walking in the overly crowded street outside. Queens Arcade is home to interesting shops, selling jewellery, clothing, food, nerdy merchandise and so on.

  • Address: Briggate, Leeds LS1 6LF
