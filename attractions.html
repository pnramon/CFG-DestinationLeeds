Kirkstall Abbey

8 March 2019

Step back into time among the haunted ruins of this historic architecture. Once home to dozens of monks, Kirkstall Abbey was founded in 1152 and remains one of the most complete Cisterian monasteries in Britain. Explore its history on the grounds itself or at the educational visitor centre. Its popular deli market is held on the last weekend of every month between May and November.

    • Leeds Art Gallery

    2 March 2019

    Explore historic and contemporary art at the Leeds Art Gallery, one of the most outstanding collections of modern British art outside London, while its Victorian clock tower has long defined the Leeds skyline. The gallery houses art and sculptures from artists: prominent, emerging, and local. Whatever your thoughts on modern art may be, Leeds Art Gallery is the place to challenge, affirm, and broaden your views.

  • Address: The Headrow, Leeds, LS1 3AA

  • Phone: +44 11 3247 8256

    • Royal Armouries

    2 March 2019

    Home to more than 75,000 world-renown objects, the Royal Armouries traces the history of weapons and armour through the milleniums. Its interactive exhibits feature medieval soldiers to modern-day armies, World War II reenactments to artifacts from the Ottoman Empire, from tournament dessage to Japanese shogun attire.
