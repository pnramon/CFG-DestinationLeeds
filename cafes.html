Where to go in Leeds when you need a cuppa

March 17, 2018

The Sociable Folk

Located near the train station, The Sociable Folk is a quiet place to enjoy a nice coffee along with some tasty treats. Serving breakfast, lunch and hot drinks; it's the perfect spot to get away from the busy city centre. Not to mention a great selection of alcoholic beverages for those who like to indulge in daytime drinking. This cafe also has a minimalist style which adds to its peaceful atmosphere.

Additional Information

  • Address: 10 Wellington Pl, Leeds LS1 4JP
  • Social Media

Temple Coffee & Donuts

With an array of vegan donuts to choose from, colourful coffess and hot chocolates and ice-cream, Temple is one of the newest craze in town. This coffee shop is a very friendly and welcoming place which has a vibrant atmoshphere. It also is a great place to try some unique flavours such as the Matcha Yuzu Float and Cola Glaze Donut.

Additional Information

  • Address: 3, Burley Pl, Leeds LS4 2AR
